About us

CarShare is an initiative of the Department of Communications, Climate Action and Environment in partnership with Corporate and Public Organisations with large scale employee numbers commuting into the greater Dublin urban area.

CarShare’s main aim is to support the government’s goal of reducing carbon emissions by 200 tonnes in the capital by 2020. In addition, the partnerships organisations, Google, Deloitte, Facebook, Microsoft, the ESB and the HSE are championing the cost savings and sociability that the initiative could present to their employees.

How CarShare works?

1. Sign-up by selecting your organisation name. Choose how you want to participate; as a driver, as a passenger, or both a driver and passenger. Enter the address you will commute from. Enter your contact details and select your shift pattern.

2. Your organisation’s CarShare group will contact you directly with Carshare options matching your preferences.

3. Choose your new CarShare colleagues. Follow the CarShare guidelines for splitting fuel costs and CarShare etiquette and begin sharing your Journey.
